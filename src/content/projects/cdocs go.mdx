## Problem

Dental practices needed a way to deliver continuing education without requiring travel to in-person events. The client wanted an interactive platform where dentists, dental assistants, and their entire practice could join live instructor-guided courses and participate in member discussions from anywhere.

## Solution

As part of a team of three developers, I built CDOCS Go, a live online education SaaS platform offering weekly interactive courses and member discussions. I chose Vue 3, Nuxt, TypeScript, and GraphQL, using Bootstrap for styling. I used Vue and GraphQL professionally for the first time on this project. It was also one of the client's earliest projects with Vue's composable API.

I generated typed GraphQL definitions with GraphQL Code Generator. Mock Service Worker powered client-side mocks for development and testing. I wrote end-to-end tests with Cypress.

## Impact

- Delivered a live education platform that let dental practices participate in weekly courses without travel.
- Helped establish Vue 3 composable patterns and typed GraphQL workflows that the team carried into future projects.
- Wrote Cypress E2E tests and MSW mocks from the start, which kept regressions low.
